Cash Booster is a classic, fast-paced slot with simple number combos and explosive bonus opportunities. It’s built for quick comprehension and sticky engagement—ideal for post-launch promos and retention pushes.

Feature Highlights

  • Respin Mechanic – If the total number shown is 0 (excluding blanks and Bonus), you get a free respin for another chance to win!
  • Wheel Bonus Game – Land 3 Bonus symbols to trigger the Cash Out Wheel, where huge multipliers await!
  • Gamble Feature – After any win, try your luck on Red or Black to double your prize—or lose it all!

金鈔狂潮 為經典風格、節奏明快的老虎機，數字組合清楚好懂，搭配爆發性的加碼機制，適合用於上線後的推廣與留存活動。
