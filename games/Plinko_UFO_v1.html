Plinko UFO is an arcade-style game where players drop a ball from a hovering UFO into a pin-filled board. The ball bounces unpredictably until it lands in one of the payout slots at the bottom. With colorful graphics and a mysterious extraterrestrial theme, this game combines luck, suspense, and instant gratification in every drop.

Feature Highlights

  • Plinko Gameplay – Drop the ball and watch it bounce through pegs to land in random multipliers ranging from 0.2x to 1000x.
  • Special Bonus Feature – Occasionally, Special Bonus spots (2x / 5x / 10x) appear. If your ball lands on one, your prize gets multiplied!
  • Auto Play Function – Enable automatic drops for a faster experience. Perfect for bulk gameplay sessions.
